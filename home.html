About Create Bastrop

Create Bastrop is a community arts platform where artists showcase their work and collectors discover original pieces. We believe every artist deserves a digital stage — and every collector deserves direct access to the people behind the work.

Our mission is simple: connect Bastrop's artists with the people who want to live with their art.
