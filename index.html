About Create Bastrop

Create Bastrop is a community arts initiative dedicated to helping local creatives be seen, connected, and celebrated. We believe every artist deserves a digital stage — whether you paint murals, write poetry, throw pottery, or strum a guitar on your front porch.

Our mission is simple: make Bastrop's creative spirit visible to the world.
